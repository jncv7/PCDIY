You should prepare all the components. This includes the following. Unboxing following:

  • MotherBoard
  • Power Supply (PSU)
  • PC Case
Check for the cables that you will need for the following:
  • MotherBoard: Typically for the CPU, 24+8 and Storage.
  • GPU: This will depend on your card. Check for the PCIE cables.
  • Fans/Case: You will be able to tell because they tend to be female cables.
Install your PSU into the case (if needed look at your manual), and route those cables throughout the case as needed.
